Cookie Policy

Last updated: 15 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Tuned Performance uses cookies and similar technologies on tunedperformance.co.uk.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser to help websites function correctly, remember preferences, and understand usage patterns.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: required for core website functionality and consent preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: optional cookies used to understand website usage and improve performance.
  • Marketing cookies: optional cookies used for campaign and attribution insights.

3. Legal Basis

Essential cookies are used based on legitimate interests for secure website operation. Optional cookies are only used where you provide consent.

4. Managing Consent

You can accept all cookies, reject optional cookies, or manage preferences via the cookie banner and the "Cookie Settings" control in the footer.

5. Third-Party Services

Some website functionality may rely on third-party providers, including form processing and embedded tools. These providers may set or read cookies under their own policies.

6. Cookie Retention

Consent preferences are stored in browser local storage until changed or cleared by you.

7. Contact

For cookie or privacy questions, contact info@tunedperformance.co.uk.
