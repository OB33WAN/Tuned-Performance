FAQs

Common Questions

Which areas do you cover?

We are based in Feltham and cover Bedfont, Ashford, Sunbury, Kingston, and Hounslow for mobile appointments.

When are you available?

We currently run weekend-only slots. Saturday and Sunday appointments are available depending on route and service type.

Do you offer ECU remap bookings?

Yes. ECU remapping is available for all major makes and models. See the live widget on the ECU remap page first.

What services can be booked mobile?

Scratch repair services, trim fitment options, exhaust tip and intake fitting, OBD diagnostics support, and MOT pickup and drop-off help can all be booked mobile.

How do I get a quote quickly?

Use the quick costing tool on the Services and Pricing page, then open the quote form or submit the contact form.

Can you help with large workshop jobs?

Yes. We can route suitable jobs to partner garages in Feltham, including Sky Tyres and MOT and Cams Car Care.

Service Guide

Definitions and Typical Figures

Service Definitions

Spot Repair
Local repair for limited paint damage without full-panel respray.
OBD Diagnosis
Fault-code scan and interpretation with practical next-step advice.
MOT Pickup and Drop-Off
Vehicle collection and return support around MOT booking.

Typical Comparisons

  • Small scratch repair: usually faster and lower cost than bumper scuff correction.
  • OBD diagnosis: lower upfront cost than replacing parts without fault data.
  • Slip-on exhaust tips: generally quicker install than diffuser and trim packages.

Typical Booking Figures

  • Weekend coverage: Bedfont, Ashford, Sunbury, Kingston, and Hounslow routes.
  • Diagnostic appointments: commonly 30 to 60 minutes.
  • Fitment labour: fixed per selected service in quick costing.
