Service Guide

Definitions, Comparisons, and Typical Figures

Quick definitions and side-by-side guidance to help you choose the right service before requesting a quote.

Service Definitions

Spot Repair
Local repair for limited paint damage without full-panel respray.
OBD Diagnosis
Fault-code scan and interpretation with practical next-step advice.
MOT Pickup and Drop-off
Vehicle collection and return support around MOT booking.

Typical Comparisons

  • Small scratch repair: usually faster and lower cost than bumper scuff correction.
  • OBD diagnosis: lower upfront cost than replacing parts without fault data.
  • Slip-on exhaust tips: generally quicker install than diffuser and trim packages.
